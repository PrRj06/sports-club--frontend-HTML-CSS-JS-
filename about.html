Welcome to Sunrise Sports Club! We are a vibrant community dedicated to promoting sports and physical fitness among students. Our club offers a variety of activities, focusing on four dynamic sports: badminton, volleyball, basketball, and boxing. Whether you're looking to compete or just have fun, there's something for everyone!

At Sunrise Sports Club, our mission is to inspire a love for sports, foster teamwork, and encourage healthy lifestyles. We believe in creating a supportive environment where athletes of all skill levels can thrive and achieve their goals.
